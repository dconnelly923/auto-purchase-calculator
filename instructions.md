# Auto purchase calculator

## Purpose

I am in the market for a new mid-sized pickup truck. I want a purpose built calculator tool that can weigh the differences between various manufacture offers, such as cash incentives and low APRs. Many offers cannot be combined, so this tool will allow me to view the pros and cons in real numbers to make educated decisions.

## Functionality

This tool shoud be able to calculate taxes, registration fees, etc. for the state of Maryland. Other parameters that should come into play are

- Purchase price
- Down payment amount
- Trade-in value
- Loan terms (in months)
- APR
- Optional additional principle payments made per month

## Vision

This should be a browser based app where I can adjust numbers and see values in realtime. Values include:

- Montly payments
- Total loan amount
- Interest paid over the life of the loan

## Final thoughts and things to consider

- I will be using this app mostly for research and education. Additionally, I will use it while negotiating at the dealership the day of purchase.
